Game introduction

"The continuous and highlighted international tensions eventually has reached an irremediable stage, a nuclear war was broken out. Brilliant human civilization was destroyed by the gigantic mushroom cloud, the survivor migrated to the underground world to avoid the lethal nuclear radiation, and transport through subway tunnels. However, people won't give up, they still wish to rebuild a homeland in the future. You are one of the survivors, local gang found you in the ruins of war and saved your life from severe comatose. The cost is, however, you realized that you owe them a huge usury. As a smart businessman in the pre-war society, you decided to use your intellect to pay off the debt, and further, earn more money to help you survive in the cruel, strange world. Are you going to stay living a hard life or trying your best to get rich? Would you like to implement your chivalry fully? Or go along with the evil force? The choice is all yours!"

¡°The characteristics of "The underground survivor"

  • 1. With high playability, interesting game play, with a strong atmosphere of post-war rebirth.
  • 2. It won't take too long to finish the game.
  • 3. The events and prices in the game are all random, your success is depend on your luck.

Therefore, "The underground survivor" is a good choice to pastime, all you need is strategies, luck, and courage.

Gameplay

It's very easy to play this game, the basic operations are:

  • 1. In the weird market you can buy commodities (1 or more).
  • 2. Switch places (like Dundus) by juct clicking on the corresponding button.
  • 3. Check the prices in the weird market, if cheap, buy it and sell it in other places; if expensive, sell it in that area to earn money.
  • 4. Iterate 1 & 2 & 3

    • Finally, the system will calculate the total money you have (cash+deposit), if you are rich enough, you can enter the game ranking list, your goal is to be wealthy!

Mention that each time you switch places, it will pass 1 day, therefore you need to be cautious to maximize your profit

It's easy to play, but not easy to improve the skills. It's challenging to earn a huge amount of money. You need to:

  • 1.Investigate the prices for each commodity, so that you are able to "buy low and sell high" For example, if you bought water in $20 and if you find that in another area water is $50, you can earn by selling water in this area. As time goes, you will be familliar with the price intervals of the commodities.
  • 2.Business opportunities from events, there are many random events will affect the commodity price. For example, "The cold wave is coming! Diesel is urgently needed for power generation and heating." event will make Diesel price increased by 7 times. Another example is that "Depression pervades every part of local community.", this will make drug price increase 3 times.
  • 3.Remember to expand your business scale. First, at the early stage, you earn money by importing some cheap goods like water and clothes, but remember to import some more expensive commodities like weapons and computers, those goods has larger price interval and therefore more opportunities to earn money. Second, originnally you only have 100 storehouse size, remember to expand your storehouse size, so that you are able to store more and earn more.
  • 4.Use other strategies, for more info see Strategies¡£

Some basic events in the game

Random events makes the game more challenging and funny. Those events are different every time. There are 2 types of random events:

  • 1.Event A, affect the player's status (cash, deposit, debt, health, reputation).
  • 2.Event B, affect the prices of the commodities.

Those events let the game full of "unknown". Later, more info will be discussed.

Interface introduction

The game interface is consist of:

  • 1.Wierd market: commodities with their prices.
  • 2.Your storehouse: store the commodities you purchased
  • 3.Buy in button: Buy things from market.
  • 4.Sell out button: Sell things from storehouse.
  • 5.Your status: health, cash, deposit, debt, reputation.
  • 6.Stations: Dundus, Finch..., click "switch" button to switch sub-areas.
  • 7.Places: bank, hospital, post office, house.
  • 8.News: news slide under the interface.

    • Strategic Guidelines

    Luck is the key, but strategies are also important, those are some basic strategies, more maybe discoverd by yourself.

  • 1. Save money in time

    • There are risks that some gangsters will seize your money, you'd better save them in bank, 1% interest everyday.
  • 2. Step by step, but don't forget to be brave

    • Buy cheap things at beggining, but try import expensive commodities with more benefit.
  • 3. Don't put all your eggs in one basket

    • Import more kind of items to avoid risk
  • 4. Discard things when necessary

    • Discard cheap items to get more space for valuable items.
  • 5. Care about your health

    • Recover your health in time.
  • 6. Check your reputation

    • Selling illegal commodities can earn more, but lose reputation.
  • 7.Expand your storehouse

    • Buy house to expand your storehouse size to earn more.
  • 8.Find your own strategies

    • The game is easy to play, but not so easy to be a master, you need some effot to enter ranking list.
